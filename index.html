Homemade Apple Pie

A Classic American Dessert

About

Apple pie is a timeless dessert beloved across the globe. With a flaky crust and warm cinnamon-spiced filling, it's perfect for holidays, special occasions, or just a cozy treat on a cool day.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 package of pie crusts (or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
  3. Place one crust in a pie pan and fill with the apple mixture.
  4. Cover with top crust, seal edges, and cut slits for steam.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let cool before serving.

Fun Fact

Although considered a quintessentially American dessert, apple pie actually originated in Europe. The phrase “as American as apple pie” became popular in the 20th century!
